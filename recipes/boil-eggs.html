How to Boil Eggs For Dummies

How to boil eggs- in case you were never taught- like me.

If you are anything like me, your parents failed to teach you many simple things growing up. How to do laundry, how to manage money, and how to cook for yourself.Don't worry, most of us have been there. The best thing to do is start with small simple things to learn and work your way up into adding to your repertoire. One super easy adn healthy snack you can make for yourself is boiled eggs.

Ingredients

  • As many eggs as desired.
  • salt

Instructions:

  1. Put eggs in a large saucepan and add water, enough to cover the eggs with.
  2. Add salt (to prevent cracking and make easier to peel) and bring water to a boil
  3. Turn off heat and cover pan with lid. Let sit for 6-12 minutes (depending on how soft ot hard you like your boiled eggs.The less time the softer, the more time, the harder.)
  4. Drain water from pot and run eggs under cool or cold tap water.
