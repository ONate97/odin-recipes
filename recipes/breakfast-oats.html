Quick Breakfast Oamteal

A quick and cheap go-to recipe for a healthy breakfast!

This recipe is quick, simple, and has endless possibilities to keep mixing it up and keep things fresh. I basically have this every single morning for breakfast adn it is hoenstly the best way to get going in the morning and have a good boost of energy

Ingredients:

  • 1/3 cup Quick oats
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinammon
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • a pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Combine oats, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt in a microwave safe cup/container.
  2. Add water and stir.
  3. Microwave on high for 1 minute and 15 seconds.
  4. Add granola and chia seeds, mix to combine.
  5. Add any other ingredients you want. Suggestions include: Almonds, fresh fruit, and nuts.
