Simple Vegan Stir-fry

This is a super easy stir fry recipe that is delicous and vegan!

It can be so hard to find good, nutritious and delicous vegan recipes that don't take ages to cook, but this recipe is defintely one of them! You only need a few ingredients and it will be ready in just about 30 minutes. This is my usual go-to recipe when I need a quick meal that will fill me up and still give me a good amount of nutrients.

Igredients you will need:

  • 14 oz container of tofu
  • 1 cup of white rice
  • 1 cup of frozen veggie stir fry mix. (usually a mix of red, green, and yellow peppers, but you can choose whatever veggies tickle your fancy.)
  • Soy Sauce
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 2-3 eggs
  • Salt
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • Pepper
  • Sesame seeds
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Boil 2 cups of salted water in medium sacue pan. Stir in rice and cook for 20 minutes, partially covered.
  2. Drain tofu package and pat dry with paper towels or a rag.
  3. Cut tofu into 1 inch cubes and toss in bowl with cornstarch, until coated.
  4. Heat oil in large skillet on med-high heat. Cook tofu cubes ~6 minutes. turning each side every few minutes for even cook. Set aside.
  5. Heat same skillet on med-high with oil. Season with a generous amount of salt and pepper. Stir in veggies and cook for 5 minutes, until soft and slightly browned, set aside.
  6. Once rice is cooked, add oil and 2-3 egg yolks to rice, season to taste, and mix together. In large skillet, heat to med-high and add rice. Drizzle soy sauce to taste and cook 3-4 minutes.
  7. plate rice, veggies, and tofu in a bowl. Sprinkle sasame seed son top.
